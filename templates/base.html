In this project, we propose a deep learning-based approach for detecting monkeypox disease using skin lesion images. We utilized a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) architecture to automatically extract features from the lesion images and classify them into two classes: monkeypox and others. Our results demonstrate that the proposed model achieved high accuracy and specificity in detecting monkeypox disease from skin lesion images, making it a promising tool for early detection of this potentially deadly disease.
